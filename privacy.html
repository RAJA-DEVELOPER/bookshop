Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly how we handle your personal information — in plain, honest language.

On This Page

1. Overview 2. Data We Collect 3. How We Use It 4. Data Sharing 5. Cookies 6. Your Rights 7. Data Retention 8. Security 9. Contact Us

Last updated:
1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Folio & Ink Ltd, registered in England and Wales (Company No. 03812499), operating from 42 Literature Lane, Mayfair, London W1K 5NE. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website, shop, and events.

2. Data We Collect

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Account registration: Name, email address, postal address, and password when you create an account.
  • Purchases: Billing and delivery addresses, payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details), and order history.
  • Enquiries & forms: Any information you voluntarily provide when contacting us, subscribing to our newsletter, or booking events.
  • Loyalty programme: Points balances, transaction history, and membership tier information.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited — collected automatically when you use our website.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information only for legitimate purposes:

  • To process orders, handle returns, and manage your account
  • To send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates)
  • To send our newsletter and reading recommendations, if you have opted in
  • To manage your loyalty programme membership and rewards
  • To personalise book recommendations and improve our services
  • To comply with our legal obligations under UK and EU law

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling in ways that have legal or significant effects on you without your explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing

We will never sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Payment processors: Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant), for secure payment handling
  • Delivery partners: Royal Mail, DPD, and international couriers — only address and contact information necessary for delivery
  • Email platform: Mailchimp, for newsletter distribution — only if you have subscribed
  • Analytics: Anonymised data only, via our self-hosted analytics platform
  • Legal authorities: Where required by law or court order

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to provide a better experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., shopping cart, session). Cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your theme, language, and display preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site. Strictly anonymised. You may opt out.

You can manage cookie preferences at any time via our Cookie Settings panel, accessible from the footer of any page.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify any inaccurate data
  • Erase your data in certain circumstances ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict how we process your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time for any processing based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@folioandinc.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary. In general:

  • Account data: Until account deletion + 12 months
  • Order data: 7 years (legal and accounting requirements)
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Until unsubscription + 90 days
  • Contact form submissions: 2 years
  • Analytics data: 13 months, anonymised thereafter

8. Security

We take data security seriously. Our measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and staff training on data handling
  • Incident response procedures in place as required by GDPR

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) within 72 hours of becoming aware.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints, please contact our Data Controller:

  • Email: privacy@folioandinc.com
  • Post: Data Privacy, Folio & Ink Ltd, 42 Literature Lane, Mayfair, London W1K 5NE
  • ICO: If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office.
