A Sanctuary for
the Written Word

Folio & Ink began in a tiny Georgian townhouse on Literature Lane, with nothing more than a passion for rare editions and a belief that a bookshop should feel like a home for stories. Twenty-five years later, we remain an independent, family-owned haven for readers, writers, and dreamers.

Every title on our shelves is hand-selected — from limited-edition poetry collections to debut novels that deserve the spotlight. We partner with independent presses and local artisans because we believe the best stories come from those who pour their heart into every page.
