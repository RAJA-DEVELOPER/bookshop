Where Every Book Finds Its Reader

Folio & Ink began as a modest dream — a corner bookshop in Mayfair with handpicked titles and a passion for the art of reading. Founded by Margaret and Edmund Folio in 1999, we have grown into one of London's most beloved literary destinations.

"We believe every book is waiting to find its reader. Our role is simply to make that meeting possible."
— Margaret Folio, Co-Founder

Over a quarter century, we have sourced rare first editions from Edinburgh to Florence, championed debut authors, and built a community around the simple joy of a great book. Our stationery collection — launched in 2007 — brings the same editorial eye to fine writing instruments, hand-bound journals, and luxury paper goods.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Six principles that have shaped Folio & Ink since the very first page was turned in our Mayfair store.

Literary Excellence

Every title we stock is carefully considered. We champion writing that endures — books that reveal something true about what it means to be alive.

Community First

Bookshops are gathering places. We foster a community of readers through events, book clubs, author readings, and shared discoveries.

Artisan Quality

From our hand-sourced rare books to our curated stationery, every product reflects an uncompromising commitment to exceptional quality.

Sustainability

We use sustainable packaging, support independent publishers, and work with suppliers who share our commitment to a greener future for publishing.

Global Perspective

Literature transcends borders. We actively seek voices from across the world, ensuring our shelves reflect the full breadth of human experience.

Curiosity & Discovery

The best reading experiences are unexpected ones. We delight in helping readers find books they never knew they needed — until they did.

Our Journey

Twenty-Five Years of Milestones

1999

The First Page

Margaret and Edmund Folio open a modest 400-square-foot bookshop in Mayfair with 800 hand-selected titles and a vision for a different kind of bookstore.

2004

The Great Expansion

We move to our current three-floor flagship, tripling our space and launching our rare books department, sourcing first editions from across Europe.

2007

Stationery is Born

The Folio & Ink Stationery Collection launches — bringing our editorial philosophy to premium journals, writing instruments, and fine paper goods.

2015

Literary Events Programme

We launch our celebrated author events and reading clubs, hosting over 200 literary evenings annually, bringing readers and writers together.

2020

Digital & Global

Our online store opens to readers worldwide, extending the Folio & Ink experience beyond Mayfair — now serving 50+ countries.

2024

25th Anniversary

We celebrate a quarter century of stories with a limited-edition collection, a major refurbishment, and our grandest literary festival to date.
